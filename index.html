What GEI Is

Global Emotional Index (GEI) is a high-level contextual indicator reflecting relative global emotional pressure derived from aggregated public signals.

GEI does not measure emotions, opinions, happiness, or mental health.

What GEI Is Not

  • Not a psychological or medical tool
  • Not a public opinion or sentiment index
  • Not a country or population ranking
  • Not a prediction, forecast, or advice system

Methodology (High-Level Overview)

GEI is derived from the aggregation and normalization of publicly observable, non-personal global signals.

These include global media activity patterns, major global events, macro-level indicators, and information density dynamics.

All signals are aggregated at a global level without country attribution or individual identification.

The resulting value represents a relative global pressure context, not an emotional state.

Privacy Policy

GEI does not collect, store, process, or infer personal data. No user accounts, cookies, tracking technologies, location data, or biometric information are used.

Media Use Guidelines

Media entities may reference GEI for contextual purposes only, with clear attribution and without misrepresentation. GEI does not provide editorial opinion or news judgment.
