Global Emotional Index (GEI)

A neutral, non-evaluative global reference indicator

The Global Emotional Index (GEI) provides aggregated, anonymized emotional pressure context at a global level. It is designed to coexist alongside economic, social, and geopolitical indicators as a contextual reference only.

What is GEI?

GEI is an experimental global reference indicator designed to visualize aggregated emotional pressure trends using publicly available, non-personal, anonymized data sources.

It does not measure happiness, sadness, approval, or disapproval of any country, government, institution, or population.

About the Global Map

Any visual global representation is symbolic and illustrative only. Country borders, colors, or regions do not represent rankings, judgments, classifications, or comparisons.

Clicking or selecting a country does not provide country-level emotional scores and does not imply monitoring, evaluation, or profiling of nations or populations.

General Disclaimer

The Global Emotional Index (GEI) is provided for informational and experimental purposes only. It is not a scientific measurement, diagnostic tool, or decision-making system.

No Political or Governmental Evaluation

GEI does not evaluate, rate, compare, or comment on governments, political systems, public policies, elections, or state actors.

No Media Directive

GEI does not constitute news, editorial opinion, analysis, or media guidance. Media outlets remain fully responsible for their own interpretations and publications.

No National or Regional Profiling

GEI does not profile countries, regions, ethnicities, cultures, or populations. No country is labeled as positive, negative, stable, unstable, safe, or unsafe.

Privacy & Data Protection

GEI does not collect personal data, biometric data, location data, or user-identifiable information. No accounts, tracking, cookies, or behavioral profiling are used.

Limitation of Liability

The creators of GEI are not responsible for decisions, actions, interpretations, or conclusions derived from the index.

Jurisdiction

GEI is designed as a global informational reference and is not subject to national emotional, psychological, or political regulatory frameworks.
