Independent offer integrity signals. No tracking. No affiliation. No influence.

What BOMBO does

BOMBO analyzes how an offer is presented to the user. It detects observable integrity signals such as artificial urgency, unclear terms, unverifiable discounts or affiliate masking.

What BOMBO does NOT do

  • No price verification
  • No legal judgement
  • No product quality assessment
  • No financial advice

BOMBO is deterministic, explainable and platform-neutral.
